About


Bran-Labs

Bran-Labs is a family business, specializing in the development of customized software products. We are located in Costa Rica and offer a wide range of high technology services that allow us to innovate, giving an extra happiness to you, through a final product that exceeds the initial expectations. Technology is a tool for which we are in love, this is how our team keeps updated with the latest technologies, standards and market protocols.


Mission

Bran-Labs is a company that focuses on designing, improving and implementing the processes of a client, providing them with technological tools that allow them to meet their needs in productive activities, as well as allowing them to be competitive in the existing market.


Planification process

We design a plan with you using an AGILE methodology. Bran-Labs uses a closely communication relationship with you in order to work together thorugh the desingning process to obtain a high satisfaction of the final software product.


Development process

Bran-Labs is committed to you and your project. Once the planning stage is completed and we have your approval, the development of the system begins. The use of close communication is such that you are part of the team. This is where you monitor, modify and validate the progress of the project; so that you adapt the project to your liking, remember that this is your project.

Release process

Once the development stage is completed, you can enjoy your original product and made to size. But this is not the end of our relationship, Bran-Labs still committed to you and your project to give you quality guarantee and support in anything you need.
